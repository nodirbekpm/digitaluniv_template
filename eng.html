Top 3 Pain Points in Higher Education

Understanding the challenges universities face every day

Subjective KPI Evaluations

Manual scoring and poor integration lead to unfair, time-consuming, and demotivating assessments

Inefficient Library Access

Paper-based workflows and limited digital access slow down resource usage and frustrate users.

Inefficient Academic Administration

Manual handling of subject differences and re-education processes leads to delays, miscommunication, and inefficient use of time and staff - frustrating students, managers, and administrators alike.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our platform

What do your pricing plans include?

All plans come with DU-KPI, DU-Lib, DU-Registration. Intermediate adds DU-Insight and standard support. Custom offers dedicated resources.

How long does it take to integrate?

Typical integration takes 2–3 weeks, including data migration and staff training.

Is my data secure?

We use AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit, and GDPR-compliant processes.

Do you offer on-premise deployment?

Yes, the Custom Plan includes on-premise install and server management.

What support channels are available?

Basic support is email only; Intermediate adds chat; Custom includes 24/7 SLAs.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely—upgrade or downgrade at any time with prorated billing.
